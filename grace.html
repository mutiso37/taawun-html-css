Our Statement of Faith

We believe in fundamental truths that guide our ministry and lives.

  • The Holy Scriptures: We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.
  • The One True God: We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • The Deity of the Lord Jesus Christ: We believe in His deity, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His vicarious and atoning death, His bodily resurrection, His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and His personal return in power and glory.
  • The Fall of Man: We believe that humanity was created good and upright, but fell from grace through voluntary transgression.
  • Salvation through Grace: We believe that salvation is received through repentance toward God and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.
  • Divine Healing: We believe in the power of divine healing, provided for through the atonement of Christ.
  • The Resurrection of the Just and the Unjust: We believe in a bodily resurrection for both the saved and the lost.
  • Eternal Life: We believe in the blessed hope of eternal life for believers and eternal punishment for the lost.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Grace Impact Ministries?

Grace Impact Ministries is a Spirit-led, Christ-centered ministry dedicated to transforming lives through the preaching of the Word, healing, youth outreach, and empowering individuals through worship, giving, and teaching.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get involved! You can attend our services and events, volunteer your time, or support our mission through donations. Please visit our 'Events' or 'Donation' sections for more details.

Do you offer programs for youth?

Yes, we have dedicated youth empowerment programs designed to nurture the next generation through mentorship, education, and spiritual development. Check our 'Ministries' section for more information.
